What is Dasvandh (or Daswand)

It is a kind of Sewa or kind of participation not only in form or money, but also in the form of sharing servcies, time for good deed and for nitnem.

This demonstrate the recognition of giving back to the giver or to the humanity (further used for needy people). its based on the three pillars of teaching of Guru Nanak Dev ji.

ਨਾਮ ਜਪੋ, ਕਿਰਤ ਕਰੋ, ਵੰਡ ਛਕੋ

  • Nam japna = Constantly chant god's name.
  • Kirat karo = Earn an honest livelihood .
  • Vand chakho = Share your earnings with others if needed.

Every sikh is expected to follow these three pillars of the teaching of Guru Nanak Dev ji
